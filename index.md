# Maryna

Backend engineer specializing in payment systems and booking flows at scale. I care about making complex systems reliable, maintainable, and easy to operate, and I enjoy understanding the full picture, from business rules to production behavior.

## About me

I studied **SMR** (Microcomputing and Network Systems Technician) from 2013 to 2015 and **DAW** (Web Application Development) from 2015 to 2017. I did my DAW internship at Kibostudios in Marbella and stayed for my first year as a professional developer. Soon after, I moved to Enreach, where I learned a lot about DDD, clean code, and writing PHP in a more thoughtful and maintainable way.

Since 2022, I have been at Docplanner / Doctoralia. There I have deepened my experience across monoliths, internal applications, and microservices that communicate through APIs and queues. My day-to-day work has been mostly backend-focused, but I also collaborate comfortably across frontend, product, operations, and adjacent stacks when needed.

## How I Work

- Strong ownership from ambiguous problem to safe delivery. I care about outcomes, not just closing tickets.
- I understand the full flow before changing critical logic. I pay attention to edge cases, especially when money, system state, or customer trust is involved.
- Pragmatic mindset: simplify first, then extend. I enjoy making systems easier to operate and codebases easier to evolve.
- Clear written communication, especially for complex changes. I document the reasoning behind a change, not only the implementation.
- Respectful collaboration across engineering, product, and operations. I have worked in teams using Scrum, Kanban, and mixed approaches.
- I enjoy onboarding people, explaining how things work, and helping teammates get up to speed.
- Fast adaptation to new domains, tools, and technologies.
